About Sofia

Sofia is an open source, personal book library application, designed for Mac OS X v10.6+. Released under the LGPL open source license.

Sofia allows you to manage your ever growing library of books. Sofia will automatically download from the internet the details of any book you import, including front cover artwork. You can arrange your books into book lists or have Sofia automatically do it for you with 'saved searches', called smart book lists. The interface is designed to resemble iTunes and so should be familiar to all.

Please feel free to download Sofia and consider contributing to the project if you are able.

Features

iTunes-like interface makes the app instantly feel comfortably familiar to anyone that has used iTunes.

Book lists can be used for organising your library. Unlimited lists can be created, just drag and drop books to easily organise your collection.

Smart book lists can be created based on search criteria. As your library changes your smart book list is always up to date.

Import books into your library direct from the web or from any list of ISBNs you can generate.

Easily maintain your shopping list of books you wish to purchase. Once purchased, easily move them into your library.

Easily browse your library by cover artwork automatically downloaded when you add a book to Sofia.
